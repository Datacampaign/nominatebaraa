Thank You for Volunteering

Your signup has been received. Here's what to expect next as part of your onboarding process:

  • 📩 You will receive a LinkedIn message confirming that you’ve been selected to lead or support a campaign effort.
  • 📁 After confirmation, you'll be sent a personalized Google Drive folder with:
    • Details of your assigned award
    • Guidelines and instructions
    • Templates and required documents
    • Submission workflow and contact points

🔔 Once you begin, keep the community updated!
Use the Shoutouts Page to share your progress, questions, and contributions.

🔔 Note on Communication:
Please take a moment to review our Communication section. As a member of the team, following our communication guidelines ensures clarity, coordination, and a respectful line of contact with Baraa and one another.

💬 Don't hesitate to ask questions or seek clarification. Our campaign thrives on collaboration and mutual support.

🎯 Now that you're in, we encourage you to start organizing around your strengths — whether it’s writing, researching, collecting documentation, or promoting the campaign.

Key Awards & Deadlines

Ashoka Fellowship

Deadline: Open — rolling nominations

Award Summary
 Ashoka builds and nurtures the world’s largest network of social entrepreneurs. Fellows receive tailored stipends (up to three years), global visibility, and lifelong peer support to scale their impact. Ashoka seeks innovators who create new patterns of social good, contributing to an “Everyone a Changemaker” world.

Conclusion
 Data With Baraa exemplifies a social entrepreneur creating profound global impact through free, high‑quality STEM education. The Fellowship’s focus on scaling pattern‑changing ideas makes this an ideal match.

Global Teacher Prize
(Varkey Foundation)

Deadline: 30 September 2025

Award Summary
 The Global Teacher Prize awards US $1 million to an exceptional educator who has transformed students’ lives and communities. For a decade it has celebrated teachers who reinvent learning and tackle local challenges with global influence.

Conclusion
 Data With Baraa embodies the Prize’s spirit through innovative STEM education that reaches a worldwide audience. With clear evidence of impact and engagement, Baraa is a powerful contender for this prestigious honour.

Yidan Prize

Status: 2025 nominations closed • 2026 cycle opens October

Award Summary
 The Yidan Prize recognises groundbreaking contributions to education in Research and Development. Each laureate receives HK $15 million cash and HK $15 million project funding (≈ US $3.8 million total) to scale their impact over three years.

Conclusion
 Data With Baraa is a standout candidate for the Yidan Prize in Education Development. His scalable, high‑impact approach to global STEM learning aligns directly with the prize’s mission of spreading effective education worldwide.

Nominations for 2025 are closed; laureates announced late September. 2026 nominations open in October.

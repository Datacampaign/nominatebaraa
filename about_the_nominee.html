About the Nominee: Baraa Khatib Salkini

Lead Data Engineering, Analytics & AI @ Mercedes-Benz AG

YouTube Educator – Data With Baraa

With over 15 years of experience in data engineering and analytics, Baraa specializes in leading large-scale Big Data, Data Lakehouse, and Business Intelligence projects. His unique ability to demystify complex data concepts has made him a standout figure in the data community.

Through his YouTube channel, Data With Baraa, he empowers over 100,000 subscribers worldwide to develop and sharpen their data skills — making advanced knowledge accessible to learners across all backgrounds.

Baraa’s approach blends strategic leadership with hands-on technical execution, building scalable data solutions that drive meaningful decision-making and operational excellence across industries.

Education

Otto-von-Guericke University Magdeburg

Master’s Degree, Data and Knowledge Engineering (2013–2015)

Master Thesis: Testing ETL Processes in Data Warehouse Systems

Languages

Arabic | German | English
